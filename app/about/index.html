About Squid Notes

Squid Notes originally started because our team thought carrying books around was a hassle. Textbooks are big and heavy, and you have to switch them for each class. Losing a book or notebook means losing all the data that you’ve organized on them.

The purpose and dream of Squid Notes is to lighten the bags of students around the world by replacing thick and heavy paper texts with light, digital textbooks.

  • Digital textbooks with interactive features.
  • Seamless integration for online and offline use.
  • Collaborative tools to help students work together.
"Squid Notes automatically syncs across all your devices so you can access your most important information anytime, anywhere. No WiFi? No problem—offline mode ensures you can continue to use Squid Notes even when the internet cuts out."
